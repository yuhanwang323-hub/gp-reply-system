I. Account Issues

A1: My information disappeared after logging in.
- Cause: You may have accidentally deleted your old account and then created a new account with the same email address.
- Solution: Please contact us via email to help you recover your original account. We recommend you keep a record of your UID, as it is the unique identifier for your account.
  
A2: I can't receive the verification code.
- Solution: Please check your spam folder first. If you still can't find it, please send your registered email address to our support email: [support@fantachat.ai].
  
A3: I accidentally deleted my account.
- Solution: We can help you recover it. However, please note that if you registered a new account with the same email after deleting the old one, the new account's data will be overwritten upon recovery.
  
A4: How to change the bound email address?
- Solution: Please send an email from both your old and new email addresses to our support email, stating your UID and the request to change the email, so we can verify your identity.
  
A5: How to find the purchase email or order number?
- Android Users:
  1. In the Google Play app, tap your profile icon > "Payments & subscriptions" > "Budget & order history."
  2. Check the email associated with your Google account. Google Play sends a confirmation email for every purchase.
  3. Visit play.google.com or payments.google.com on a computer to view your order history.
  4. Important: We need the Google order number that starts with GPA. Order numbers from banks or other payment apps (like Alipay, WeChat Pay) are not useful.
- iOS Users:
  1. Open the App Store and tap your profile icon in the top right.
  2. Tap "Purchase History" to see all orders.
  3. Check the email associated with your Apple ID. Apple sends a receipt for every purchase.
    
A6: Can I change the gender I selected during registration?
- Policy: We're sorry, but changing the registered gender is not currently supported. Please choose carefully during registration.

M1: The model forgot my name or our chat history.
- Cause: Due to the AI's memory limitations, some early information may be forgotten after a long conversation.
- Suggestion: You can use the "Pin" feature to save important information or remind the AI during the conversation.
  
M2: The chat contains unsupported languages or a mix of languages.
- Solution: If the character's reply has language issues (e.g., mixed languages), try regenerating the response starting from the first problematic message. This usually solves the problem. If the issue persists, please send a screenshot of the relevant chat to our support email. We are continuously working to optimize multi-language support.
  
M3: Why are there so many content restrictions?
- Explanation: To comply with Google Play's platform policies, we must restrict content related to pornography, violence, etc. The strictness of these restrictions depends on changes in platform policy.
  
M4: The bot is using offensive language.
- Solution: Please send a screenshot of the relevant chat to our support email. We will investigate and address the issue immediately.
  
M5: Why does the web version keep asking to delete my account?
- Explanation: The web version is currently testing an account deletion tool for some users. For the full product experience, we recommend using our mobile app.
  
M6: Can I turn off the profanity filter?
- Policy: We're sorry, but the profanity filter cannot be turned off. We must maintain a content moderation system to comply with Google Play's platform policies.
  
M7: Can I generate nude images or overly explicit text?
- Policy: We're sorry, but we cannot generate nude images or provide overly explicit text content. This is to comply with Google Play's platform policies.
